Research & Background

Jayashree Nair is an Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) at the School of Computing, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri campus. Her work sits at the intersection of linguistics and computer science, with a particular focus on making technology work better for Indian languages.

Her research spans machine translation, morphological analysis, transliteration, text-to-speech systems, optical character recognition, and NLP tools for classical and regional Indian languages including Sanskrit, Hindi, Bengali, and Malayalam.

Beyond core NLP, she has contributed to humanitarian technology projects — designing IoT-based healthcare solutions and community systems for rural villages in Bihar and Himachal Pradesh.
